About me

I’m a web developer who’s been working mostly in Drupal since 2010, which means that I’m fluent in Drupal 6 and upwards. I’ve also done some work in Symfony and now that Drupal 8 is based on that framework I get to enjoy the sweet synergy juices. What I love doing the most is building and maintaining systems that are meant to last for a long time. Quality in terms of performance, stability, scalability and extensibility are very important motivators for me and those tend to be more valued the longer the intended lifespan of a system is.

Apart from backend development in PHP I’m also comfortable with Python as well as Node.js. Right now I’m especially interested in distributed computing and ventured into Go for that reason. I’m also fascinated by AI, especially evolutionary computing. The potential there is just huge. I also enjoy frontend development, particularly user interfaces.
