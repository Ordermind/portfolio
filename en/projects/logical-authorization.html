Logical Authorization

I have created a series of plugins for Symfony that integrates my permissions library in order to create a more flexible, convenient and consistent developer experience. One example of a feature that could be very useful is the ability to export all permissions in a simple way so that they can also be used client-side.

Here is an article that explains the motivation behind the plugin: https://medium.com/@ordermind/better-authorization-for-symfony-4-c958e9c6a410

Github link: https://github.com/ordermind/symfony-logical-authorization-bundle
