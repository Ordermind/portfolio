Sluta Leta Bil

The car dealer Sluta Leta Bil needed a smoother administration experience for displaying their cars. They used the popular site Byt Bil to display their cars and wanted the website to import them from there so that they didn’t need to add the car data in more than one place. I built a new website for them in PHP / Drupal 7, which has great support for integrations with external systems.

As part of the backend and frontend work I built an integration with Byt Bil to achieve the automatic import of the cars. I also built an aide for adding stamps to images in the case that the car was part of a campaign, since they lacked such a tool.

Thanks to a thorough job with SEO on the site, the traffic also increased by 100% after the new website was launched, which was a nice bonus.
