Louis De Geer

Louis De Geer supplies facilites for concert and congress, and needed a website that tied together their operations in a neat and user-friendly way. One of the things they asked for was a smooth, semi-automatic tool for handling the intermission menus to their events in order to minimize the manual administration.

I built the backend and frontend in PHP / Drupal 7, and apart from the intermission menu tool I also added integrations with Facebook, Spotify and Ticnet. A search form for events assured easily findable events, and an encompassing multilingual build made the whole site available for international visitors.
